Does using a temporary email violate Facebook's terms of service?

Facebook's terms don't specifically prohibit temporary email addresses. Their primary concern is authentic identity and preventing spam or fraud. Can I recover my Facebook account if I lose access?

Facebook's terms allow only one personal account per person. Creating multiple accounts violates their policy regardless of which email addresses you use. Understanding the difference between legitimate secondary accounts and policy violations is important before creating additional profiles.
