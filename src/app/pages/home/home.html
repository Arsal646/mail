What is a Temporary Email? (One Time Inbox)

A temporary email, also known as a disposable or one-time inbox, is a short-lived email address you can use to sign up for websites, download files, or receive verification codes without sharing your real email.

It works just like a normal inbox, but it automatically deletes after some time. That means no spam, no ads, and no unwanted tracking. You stay private while still being able to access what you need online.
