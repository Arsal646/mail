How TempMail Works

Create disposable email addresses in seconds. No signup required.

1

Go to Homepage

Your temporary email is ready immediately

When you visit our homepage, you'll see:

  • Your temporary email address already generated
  • An empty inbox ready to receive emails
  • Copy button next to your email address
  • Refresh button to check for new emails

The homepage shows your temporary email address and empty inbox.

2

Copy Email Address

Use your temporary email for sign-ups

Click the copy button next to your email address. The email is now copied to your clipboard.

Use this temporary email address when signing up for websites, services, or newsletters.

Click the copy button to copy your temporary email address.

3

Receive Emails

Emails appear in your temporary inbox

Keep the TempMail tab open. Emails sent to your temporary address will appear here.

The inbox shows:

  • Sender email address
  • Email subject
  • Time received

Your inbox displays received emails with sender details.

4

Read Messages

View email content and attachments

Click on any email to view its full content.

You can:

  • Read the complete message
  • Download attachments
  • Copy verification codes
  • View sender information

View full email content and download attachments.

5

Create New Email

Generate fresh addresses when needed

Click "Generate New Email" to create a new temporary address.

Use this when you need:

  • A different email for another service
  • To start with a fresh inbox
  • Multiple temporary addresses

Create new temporary email addresses with one click.
